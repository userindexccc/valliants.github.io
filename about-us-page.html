About Us

The Sports Development Office is a sports organization that every bonafide students in the City College of Calamba are allowed to join. We offer different types of sports like basketball, volleyball, badminton, board games and cheerdance. We are committed to enhancing the skills of our players and show sportmaship and great teamwork.

COMPETENCE COMMITMENT CHARACTER

MISSION

We, athletes must inculcate the core values of CCC and carry the undying perseverancee of heroes to greater heights motivated by the desires and aspirations of every athletes in giving pride and honor to the College and the City.

VISION

CCC VARSITY TEAM envisions itself to be premiere camp of disciplined, loyal, hardworking, dignified and sports minded athletes that will carry the name of the college and Calamba City in the local, national, and international arena.
